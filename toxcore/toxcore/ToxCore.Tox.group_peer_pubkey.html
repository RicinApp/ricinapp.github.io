group_peer_pubkey

Description:

public int group_peer_pubkey (int group_number, int peer_number, out uint8[] public_key)

Copy the public key of peernumber who is in groupnumber to public_key. public_key must be TOX_PUBLIC_KEY_SIZE long.

Returns:

0 on success, -1 on failure
